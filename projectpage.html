Lazy River and Garden Supply

In this project we were tasked with building a three-page website for the fictional small business "Lazy River Nursery and Garden Supply." Utilizing the instruction received during Week 1 of the program, we were to write the code for a homepage, an in-stock page, and a general information page. Specific tasks included providing accurate links to navigate between the different pages as well as images given to us in the project scenario.
